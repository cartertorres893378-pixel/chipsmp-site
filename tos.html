Chip SMP Terms of Service

By joining Chip SMP, you agree to follow all rules listed below. Failure to follow these rules may result in punishment including mutes, bans, or permanent removal from the server.

1. General Rules

  • No hacking, cheating, or use of unfair mods (X-ray, kill aura, etc.)
  • No exploiting bugs or glitches
  • No impersonating staff or other players
  • Respect all players and staff

2. Chat Rules

  • No racism, hate speech, or discrimination
  • No spamming or advertising (unless allowed)
  • No harassment or threats
  • Keep chat appropriate

3. Gameplay Rules

  • No griefing or stealing
  • No lag machines or server crashing builds
  • Do not abuse game mechanics unfairly
  • Follow staff instructions at all times

4. Punishments

Punishments may vary depending on severity:

  • Warnings
  • Temporary mutes or bans
  • Permanent bans for serious offenses

5. Changes

These rules can be updated at any time without notice. It is your responsibility to stay updated.

6. Disclaimer

We are not responsible for lost items, progress, or data. Play at your own risk.
